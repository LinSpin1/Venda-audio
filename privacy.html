Privacy Policy for Learn Tshivenda with Mutodi

Last updated: January 2025

Learn Tshivenda with Mutodi (“the App”), developed by Dalton Digital, is designed to provide users with a guided language-learning experience. This Privacy Policy describes how the App collects, uses, and protects user information.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect personally identifiable information from users.

However, the following may be collected automatically:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App performance data
  • Anonymous analytics (crashes, loading times)
  • Ad interaction data through Google AdMob

No names, phone numbers, email addresses, or personal identity data are collected.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following external services, which may collect anonymous data for their normal functionality:

  • Google AdMob – for banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads
  • Google Play Services – for analytics and performance

These services may automatically collect:

  • Approximate location (for ad relevance)
  • Device identifiers
  • App usage and performance logs

3. How We Use Data

Any collected data is used only for:

  • Improving app performance
  • Ensuring a smooth learning experience
  • Displaying relevant advertising

Dalton Digital does not sell, rent, or share user data.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App is safe for children but may display age-appropriate ads through Google AdMob. No personal child data is collected.

5. Security

We follow industry-standard practices to protect data. No personal information is stored by the App.

6. User Rights

Because the App does not store identifiable information, users do not need to request deletion or correction of personal data.

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
